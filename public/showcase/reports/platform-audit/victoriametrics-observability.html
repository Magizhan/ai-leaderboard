Root Cause: High-Cardinality Labels

LabelUnique ValuesImpact
route_id2,8618.3M series
num_stops~105.5M series
hour242.5M series
le (histogram)15-20Multiplier on all above
pod1,754Per-pod cardinality

Cardinality Formula

route_id (2,861) x num_stops (~10) x hour (24) x le (~15) x pods (10) = 100M+ potential series

Queries hit the 5M series vmstorage search limit and fail.

Fix: Drop route_id + num_stops from histograms. Use recording rules for city-level aggregates. Reduces 10.1M to ~1.5M series.
