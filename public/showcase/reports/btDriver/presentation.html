Issue #1 · Existential Threat

The Pricing Paradox

The Problem

  • Zero commission, yet fares are 25-50% higher than Uber/Ola/Rapido
  • 182 complaints in February alone
  • Competitor mentions: Uber (247x), Ola (170x), Rapido (119x)
  • Directly contradicts the cooperative brand promise
  • Users default to "scam" and "looting" language

Why This Is Existential

The entire brand promise is "no middleman, lower fares." When users discover fares are higher, it destroys trust permanently. This is the #1 reason for 1-star reviews.

"For a ride that cost 400 in Bharat Taxi, it cost 270 for the same ride in Uber" — 1-star review
"This is not a SAHKARI model at all, but it's a Looting Model in the name of the SAHKARI model... Go and learn from AMUL" — 1-star review
"Being government supported app, fares are 30-40% higher than Uber. If drivers get 80% of fare, why should the fare be so high?" — 3-star review
Issue #2 · Critical

GPS & Location Failures

108 complaints in February

  • Delhi-Gurgaon cross-boundary bug — specific, reproducible
  • No GPS accuracy filtering — accepts readings >100m off
  • No battery optimization guidance for Xiaomi/Oppo/Vivo
  • Location service killed by Android battery saver
  • Only detects stale location on app resume (60s threshold)

Zero Monitoring

Only 4 Prometheus metrics exist on the driver side. No GPS accuracy metrics. No merchant-level labels — Bharat Taxi is invisible in production monitoring.

"The map is so stupid, it's constantly showing the wrong block despite placing the exact location pin" — 1-star review

Config Bug Found

Bharat Taxi cities Somnath, Rajkot, Dwarka have Delhi coordinates hardcoded in v1Config.json — maps center on the wrong city entirely.

Issues #3 & #4

Availability Gaps & Driver Behaviour

Service Availability — 99 complaints

  • Users in Mumbai, Kolkata, Bangalore, Pune can't find rides
  • App collects registration details before revealing no coverage
  • Creates permanent churn — users never return
  • 517% complaint increase from Dec to Jan
"What's the point in having the app if any prime area in Pune is not serviceable?" — 1-star review

Driver Cancellations — Avg 1.65★

  • Drivers accept then drive in opposite direction
  • Demand extra cash outside the app
  • Cancel for better offers on Uber/Ola (dual-apping)
  • Lowest-rated category across all months
"Both drivers confirmed via call and app but then drove in the opposite direction while forcing me to wait. Had I trusted them, I would've missed my train" — 1-star review
Code Findings

Critical Bugs Found in Code

🔇 Android Notifications Silently Dropped

In ListenerComponent.tsx:83-90:

if (Platform.OS === 'android') return;

3 ride notification types (NEW_RIDE_AVAILABLE, CLEARED_FARE, CANCELLED_SEARCH_REQUEST) are silently ignored on Android. Drivers are missing rides.

📍 Wrong City Coordinates

In v1Config.json, Bharat Taxi cities Somnath, Rajkot, and Dwarka all have Delhi coordinates hardcoded. Maps center on Delhi instead of the actual city.
