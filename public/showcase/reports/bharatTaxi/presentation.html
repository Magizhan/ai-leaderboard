05

8-Week Execution Plan

05 / Roadmap
Week 1-2
Deploy Fixes + Review Response Blitz
Deploy all 8 implemented fixes behind feature flags • Deploy Sentry crash reporting • Connect Prometheus → Grafana dashboards • 100% reply rate on 1-star reviews (12-hr SLA) • Publish fare rate cards for all cities
Week 3-4
Driver Supply + Monitoring
Phase in cancellation penalties (warnings first, then cooldowns) • Reduce refund SLA from 15-20 days to 5 days • Driver heat maps + demand forecasting • In-app live chat (basic chatbot + human escalation)
Week 5-6
City Expansion Wave 1
Launch Mumbai — highest demand (32 review mentions), BluSmart void • Launch Hyderabad — auto union publicly welcomed BT, 1.2L auto-rickshaws • MapMyIndia fallback geocoder for tier-2/3 location autocomplete
Week 7-8
Payment + Support Scaling
Launch Pune — piggybacks Mumbai partnerships • Add card/wallet payment (Paytm MoU signed Feb 12) • Real-time fare meter visible during ride • Ride scheduling for airport and early morning
Critical Path: Monitoring (Week 1) must precede city expansion (Week 5). Cancellation penalties (Week 3) must precede Mumbai launch. Feature flags on all changes enable rollback within minutes.
