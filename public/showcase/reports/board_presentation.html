Why It's a Distraction

  • Indian startups have poor global track record (Ola exited UK, AU)
  • EU regulation complexity (GDPR, local licensing)
  • BECKN international adoption still pilot-only
  • Engineering overhead: multi-currency, multi-language

Why Keep It (at Low Cost)

  • BECKN + Google Cloud (Beckn Onix) = DPI narrative
  • Cost is trivial (₹0.25 Cr / year)
  • "Indian DPI going global, like UPI" = 100x narrative multiplier
  • Amsterdam OAN pilot could create organic pull
Verdict: Keep as <2% resource R&D. Don't hire for it. Don't pitch it as a line item. If organic traction appears, accelerate in CY27.
