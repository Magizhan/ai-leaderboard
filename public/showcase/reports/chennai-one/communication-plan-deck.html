Our Approach: Issue-Specific, Not Generic

Generic (Don't Do This)

"We've improved the app! Update now for a better experience."

Same message to everyone. Low engagement. Users feel unheard.

Contextual (Our Approach)

"Your refund of ₹24 for the metro booking on Mar 5 has been processed. We've fixed the payment system."

References THEIR issue. Shows we know and care. Builds trust.

Every user gets communication about the specific problem THEY experienced

Communication Guidelines

Do NOT Say

  • Technical terms (SDK, API, atomicity)
  • Partner blame (CMRL, CRIS, Juspay)
  • Version numbers ("Update to v3.3.30")
  • Over-apologizing

DO Say

  • Value-first ("Payments are 4x faster")
  • Reference THEIR issue ("Your refund of ₹24...")
  • Clear CTA ("Book Now", "View Refund")
  • Social proof ("150K+ commuters")
