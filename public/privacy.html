Privacy Policy

Claude Usage Sync — Chrome Extension
Last updated: March 16, 2026

What We Collect

The extension reads the following data from your claude.ai/settings/usage page:

  • Your Claude username (from the profile button)
  • Session and weekly usage percentages
  • Session and weekly reset timer information

This data is sent to our leaderboard API at leaderboard.magizhan.work to display team usage rankings.

Authentication

The extension reads a Cloudflare Access authentication cookie (CF_Authorization) from the leaderboard domain to authenticate API requests. This cookie is set after you sign in with your Google Workspace account (@juspay.in or @nammayatri.in). No passwords are collected or stored.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • No browsing history, web activity, or page content outside of claude.ai/settings/usage
  • No personal communications, emails, or chat messages
  • No financial, health, or location data
  • No keystrokes, clicks, or mouse activity

Data Storage

Usage data is stored in Cloudflare KV (key-value storage) associated with your leaderboard user profile. Local preferences (team selection, sync schedule) are stored in Chrome's local storage on your device.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, transfer, or share your data with any third parties. Data is only used to display the team leaderboard at leaderboard.magizhan.work, accessible to authenticated @juspay.in and @nammayatri.in users.

Data Retention

Usage history is retained for up to 500 data points per user. You can request deletion of your data by contacting the leaderboard administrator.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the leaderboard administrator via your internal team channels.
